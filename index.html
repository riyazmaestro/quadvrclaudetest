Quad Flight XR

Fly a physically-simulated quadcopter in your living room.

  1. Put on your headset and stand in the middle of clear floor space.
  2. Tap Enter AR and allow camera / spatial data access.
  3. Left stick: throttle (up/down) + yaw (left/right). Right stick: pitch/roll.
  4. Grip (either hand) arms/disarms the motors. A toggles ACRO/ANGLE mode. Y resets the drone to your feet.
  5. Squeeze both triggers at once any time to instantly cut the motors.

Look around the room to map your flying space…
