Quad Flight XR

Fly a physically-simulated quadcopter in your living room.

  1. Put on your headset and stand in the middle of clear floor space.
  2. Tap Enter AR and allow camera / spatial data access.
  3. Walk the edge of your flying space (around furniture too) and pull the right trigger at each corner to mark it (3+ corners). Walk back to your first point and mark it again to close the boundary.
  4. Left stick: throttle (up/down) + yaw (left/right). Right stick: pitch/roll.
  5. Right trigger engages/disengages the motors. A resets the drone to the center of the room. X re-walks the room boundary. Y toggles ACRO/ANGLE mode.
  6. Squeeze both grips at once any time to instantly cut the motors.
